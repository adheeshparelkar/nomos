Keyholders

When entering

Access is via the front door on Venables street. The lock is the same as at Cook so just pretend you are in the hallway. The new code is {{innerdoor}}.

The “is VHS open bot” is now located on the admin cabinet by the small office in the main area if you would like to open the space to non keyholders.


To open, punch in the current door access number: {{innerdoor}}

The lock is now unlocked and you can turn the thumbturn clockwise to unlock the door.


When leaving

Follow the space closing checklist here

To lock the inner door after you have left, press the Schlage button on the top, then rotate the thumbturn (counter-clockwise) away from the hinges.

Please test the door to confirmed that it is closed and locked properly.
